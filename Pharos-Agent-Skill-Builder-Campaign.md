# HighTower Joins the Pharos Agent Center Skill Builder Campaign

HighTower is joining the **Pharos Agent Center Skill Builder Campaign** to support developers building the next generation of AI-native onchain tooling.

As part of the campaign, HighTower will provide infrastructure support, ecosystem exposure, media distribution, and additional builder rewards for selected participants building Skills for the Pharos Agent Center.

The Pharos Agent Center represents an important step toward AI-powered blockchain interactions, enabling AI Agents to execute real onchain actions through structured Skills and natural language interfaces.

We believe infrastructure, accessibility, and developer support will play a critical role in the growth of AI-native ecosystems — and we want to help builders experiment, launch, and scale faster.

---

## What HighTower Will Provide

Selected builders participating through the HighTower support track may receive access to infrastructure resources, ecosystem support, and additional distribution opportunities.

### Dedicated RPC & Agent RPC Infrastructure

HighTower will provide dedicated infrastructure support for selected participants, including:

- Dedicated RPC endpoints
- Agent-optimized RPC access
- High-performance blockchain connectivity
- MCP endpoints for AI-agent integrations
- Stable infrastructure environments for testing and development

The goal is to help developers focus on building useful Skills without dealing with infrastructure limitations during development and testing.

---

### Indexing & Data Layer Support

Some projects may also receive access to HighTower indexing and data infrastructure adapted to their specific use case.

Depending on the project requirements, this may include:

- Indexed blockchain data access
- Data endpoints optimized for AI-agent interactions
- Custom data routing and structured query support

Infrastructure allocation will depend on the technical relevance and quality of the submitted project.

---

### Media & Distribution Support

HighTower will actively support ecosystem visibility for selected projects. This includes:

- Social media coverage
- Technical breakdowns
- Skill showcases
- Builder spotlights
- Ecosystem reports
- Community exposure

The best projects may also receive participation in our **Builder Storytelling Layer** initiative — where we highlight standout teams, explain their architecture and use cases, and help showcase them to the broader Web3 ecosystem.

---

## Additional Builder Rewards

To further support developers building during the campaign, HighTower will additionally provide:

- **3 Cursor Pro+ accounts** (1 month)
- **3 Claude Pro accounts** (1 month)

These rewards will be distributed to selected builders based on:

- Technical quality
- Originality
- Usefulness
- Ecosystem relevance
- Overall contribution to the Pharos Agent Center ecosystem

---

## How to Participate Through HighTower

Builders can already submit applications through the HighTower Builder Form before the official support program launch.

**Application form:** [https://www.htw.tech/builders/form](https://www.htw.tech/builders/form)

When submitting the form, participants **MUST** include `[Pharos]` before the project name.

**Example:**

```
[Pharos] Wallet Analytics Skill
```

> Applications without the `[Pharos]` prefix may not be included in the support program.

---

## Participation Requirements

To be eligible for HighTower support and additional rewards, participants must:

- Follow HighTower social channels
- Follow Pharos social channels
- Publish at least one Twitter/X post about their Skill, development progress, or participation in the campaign
- Include screenshots, demos, or technical explanations whenever possible
- Submit an original and functional project
- Use public or reviewable code repositories

We strongly encourage builders to share development updates publicly during the campaign.

---

## What Kind of Projects We Want to Support

We are especially interested in:

- AI-agent infrastructure tooling
- Onchain automation Skills
- Wallet intelligence systems
- DeFi interaction layers
- Analytics and monitoring tools
- Developer utilities
- Agent execution systems
- Smart contract interaction layers
- Ecosystem indexing tools
- Autonomous onchain workflows

Both beginner and advanced builders are welcome.
